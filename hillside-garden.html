LANDSCAPE DESIGN

Hillside Garden

A terraced landscape project that transforms a steep hillside into a series of gardens, viewpoints, and outdoor living spaces.

Project Overview

The Hillside Garden project reimagines a challenging sloped site into a sequence of interconnected outdoor spaces that celebrate the natural terrain rather than working against it.

Through carefully designed terraces, pathways, and planting strategies, the project creates an immersive landscape experience with panoramic views and year-round beauty.

Location

Nagarkot, Nepal

Year

2022

Area

2500 sqm

Type

Landscape Design

CONCEPT

Working With Nature

The design embraces the site's natural slope, transforming elevation changes into opportunities for unique outdoor experiences. Terraced gardens, stone pathways, native planting, and viewing platforms create a landscape that feels both structured and organic while preserving the character of the hillside. The project focuses on sustainability, biodiversity, and creating meaningful connections between people and nature.
