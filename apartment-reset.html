INTERIOR DESIGN

Apartment Reset

A complete transformation of an urban apartment into a warm, functional and contemporary living space.

Project Overview

Apartment Reset is a full interior renovation project focused on improving spatial flow, storage efficiency, and everyday comfort.

The design embraces natural materials, soft lighting, and carefully curated furniture to create a calm and elegant environment for modern family living.

Location

Lalitpur, Nepal

Year

2024

Area

145 sqm

Type

Residential Interior

CONCEPT

Modern Living Reimagined

The project focuses on simplicity, functionality, and comfort. Existing partitions were removed to create an open-plan living experience while maximizing natural light throughout the apartment. A neutral palette of wood, stone, and soft fabrics creates a timeless interior that feels both modern and welcoming.
