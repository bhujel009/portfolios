RESIDENTIAL ARCHITECTURE

Courtyard House

A contemporary residence designed around light, landscape, and human experience.

Project Overview

The Courtyard House is a private residence designed to create a strong connection between indoor living spaces and nature.

The central courtyard becomes the heart of the home, bringing natural light and ventilation into every room while creating a peaceful outdoor retreat.

Location

Kathmandu, Nepal

Year

2024

Area

320 sqm

Type

Residential

CONCEPT

Design Philosophy

The project focuses on creating a balance between privacy and openness. The central courtyard acts as the organizing element, allowing daylight, natural ventilation, and visual connections throughout the house. Natural materials such as timber, stone, and exposed concrete create warmth and timeless character while maintaining a contemporary architectural language.
